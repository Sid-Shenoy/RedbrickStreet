Product Name: Redbrick Street
Product Description: A first person zombie shooter game.
Platform Details:
- Will use Vite, TypeScript, and Babylon.js
- Must be accessible from web browser.

Environment:
- The setting is just a single street (inspired by Williamson Drive).
- The entire street will be surrounded by a tall brick wall to eliminate boundaries.
- But it must be detailed to the point that it becomes similar to reality.
- Each house will have two floors.
- There are no vehicles.

Background:

Zombies have invaded Redbrick Street! The player must kill them all, while collecting resources and staying alive.

Controls:
The player will use the WASD keys to move, Shift to sprint, and Space to jump. The mouse (left-click) will be used to attack.

Non-functional requirements:
NFR-01: The game must not require any credits or fees due to licensing (only use freely available resources).
NFR-02: A user must be able to open and play the game directly in the web browser.
